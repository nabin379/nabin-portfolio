Gosaikunda Vlog


Gosaikunda, located in the Langtang National Park of Nepal, is a sacred alpine freshwater lake that draws Hindu and Buddhist pilgrims seeking to cleanse themselves of sins by immersing in its holy waters. At 4,380 meters above sea level, the lake is surrounded by snow-capped mountains and rugged terrain, including Langtang Lirung, Ganesh Himal, and Manaslu. The lake is fed by snow and glaciers and empties into the Trishuli River through the Gosaikunda Dam.

Besides being a pilgrimage site, Gosaikunda is a popular trekking destination, offering a 7-10 day trek that meanders through picturesque valleys, dense forests, and remote mountain villages. Along the journey, trekkers can marvel at the stunning Himalayan views and immerse themselves in the fascinating culture and traditions of the local Tamang and Sherpa communities
